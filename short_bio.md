Dr. Stephen Roddy is a media engineering researcher specialising in sonification within Sound and Music Computing (SMC). He lectures at University College Cork (UCC) and holds a PhD in Engineering from Trinity College Dublin. He is currently focused on sonification for the Internet of Sounds (IoS) and ethical approaches to artificial intelligence & machine learning (AI/ML) in creative audio. His work is informed by Cybernetics, Human-Computer Interaction, and Radical Embodied Cognitive Science.
